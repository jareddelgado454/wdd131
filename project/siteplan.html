Site Plan: AutoFix Solutions

Site Name

AutoFix Solutions

I chose this name because the site focuses on helping people get fast and reliable car repair services, offering guidance, tips, and booking options.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with helpful automotive maintenance information, quick service booking, recommended tools, and guidance to keep their vehicles in good condition.

Scenarios

  • “How can I schedule a mobile mechanic to check my car?”
  • “What basic tools do I need to maintain my car at home?”

Color Schema

Below are the chosen site colors and how they will be used:

  • Main Blue for headings and buttons
  • Accent Gold for highlights and important elements

Typography

The selected site font is:

  • Poppins — used for all text.
